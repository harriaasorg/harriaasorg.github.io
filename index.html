Never forget anything from the store again

About

GList in a helpful companion for you when you go to the store. You can create your own lists, use default templates or create your own. The app is highly customizable. The main problem for us was forgetting to buy things from the store and keeping within a limit. Our solution is simple yet very effective and easy to use. Our app can be used by everyone.
