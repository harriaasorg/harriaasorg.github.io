This is a game where you play as a schizophrenic person,
who is afraid of trick or treaters and you need to defend your house from them

Game mockup

Core loop

Defend your house

Put defenses around your house (eg.windows) to defend it from trick or treaters

Collect candies

Move around your house to collect candies, which were dropped by the trick or treaters. The more candy you get the better

Buy different weapons and defences

With the candy you get, you can buy different weapons and defences to defend you and your house
